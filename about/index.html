work / about

I am a 3D Artist and Shader Developer from Taiwan. I love making things visually appealing since I was a child. After receiving my B.A. in Creative Design from National Yunlin University of Science and Technology, I began my career in 2013 at a watch manufacturing company in Taiwan, where I design watch faces and packaging. My journey led me to explore marketing, where I was passionate about desiging various materials such as brand identities, websites, brochures and videos with the goal of positively expressing the business's identity and appealing to potential partners and customers.

After extensive experience in marketing, I wanted to explore challenges that allow me to express my art and design skills through the creation of games and narratives.

In 2021, I transitioned to leading the art direction for a game project. In this role, I am responsible for everything from hands-on creation of 3D artworks and environments to optimizing visual effects and rendering in Unity. I enjoy getting involved in the groundwork for projects.

在市場營銷方面獲得豐富經驗後，我希望挑戰通過創作遊戲和敘事來表現我的藝術和設計技能。

2021年，我轉而擔任一個遊戲項目的藝術指導。在這個職位中，從親自創建3D模型和環境到進行Unity中的視覺效果和渲染的優化，我負責各方面具創造力的工作。
