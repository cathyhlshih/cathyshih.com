Cathy Shih
work / about

I started my art and design journey in 2013 at a watch manufacturing company in Taiwan, where I worked as a Associate Watch Designer. Following this role, I utilized my design skills in various sales and marketing initiatives, including websites development, brand identities creation, and the designs of presentations and marketing materials.

Although I found fulfillment in the creativity and challenges of business-oriented work, I felt like exploring challenges that allow me to express my art and design skills through the creation of games and narratives.

In 2021, I transitioned to leading the art direction for an MMORPG project. In this role, I am responsible for everything from hands-on creation of 3D artworks and environments to optimizing visual effects and rendering in Unity. I enjoy getting involved in the groundwork for projects.

2013年，我開始了我的藝術和設計之旅。首先，我在台灣一家手錶製造公司擔任手錶設計師助理。之後，我將我的設計技能應用於各種銷售和市場營銷活動，包括網站開發、品牌形象創建以及簡報和營銷材料的設計。

儘管我在以商業為導向的工作中帶來的創造力和挑戰得到滿足感，我更想要挑戰透過創作遊戲和故事敘事來表達我的藝術和設計技能。

到了2021年，我決定轉型，開始領導一個大型多人在線角色扮演遊戲（MMORPG）項目的藝術指導工作。在這個職位中，我負責從親自創建3D模型和環境到進行Unity中的視覺效果和渲染的優化，涵蓋了各方面具創造力的工作。
